Favorites

Reliable equipment is definitely an important invesment for Graphic Design. By using the right tools, the force of our talent and creativity can be multiplied exponentially.
可靠的設備對於視覺設計來說，是個十分重要的長期投資，對的工具將會讓我們的才華和創造力發揮到極限。
Here is my list of the best software tools in the field today.
以下是我推薦的設計軟體。

Adobe Photoshop


When you want to edit a photograph, Adobe Photoshop is just perfect for you. Now part of the Adobe Creative Cloud, the primary disadvantage is that there is no perpetual license for this app any more. Subscription prices are reasonably affordable at $9.99 per month, but users are forced into a plan that they may not necessarily use frequently.
Adobe Photoshop非常適合編輯加工照片或影片，訂閱價格還算合理，每月9.99美元，但因為最近制度規定上的改變，用戶被迫參與不太實用的方案。
The tools that are available in Adobe Photoshop are suitable for the web and for mobile responsiveness. The interface is very user-friendly so that anyone can begin to edit their photographs with ease.
Adobe Photoshop中提供的工具適用於網頁以及手機頁面，界面十分地人性化，讓每個認都可以用各種設備輕鬆編輯照片。
Instead of spending hours trying to hunt down stock images that aren’t going to cost you a fortune to use, Photoshop has a completely integrated stock library that will help you create the perfect image or perfect video.
同時其擁有一個完整的素材庫存，可以提供一些精緻的圖像或視頻，讓設計者不用花費大量時間，在網上尋找合適的素材。

Canva


Canva is mostly free. Canva has many fonts and graphics that don’t cost anything to use. You can make an account and do not have to pay for a subscription.
Canva主要的功能都是免費的，有許多不需要花錢就可以使用的字體和圖形，只要註冊一個帳戶，無需付費，就可以享用蠻齊全的編輯功能。 Canva has preset sizes and templates for social media posts, postcards, posters, cards, flyers, etc. Having preset options can save someone so much time when trying to figure out specific dimensions for their creation. Just click on the type of content you want to create and they already have everything sized and designed for you.
此外，Canva提供許多不同種類風格的範本，包含明信片，海報，卡片，傳單等的預設大小和模板，可以節省糾結實際尺寸的時間，只需點擊所需的內容類型，就可以在適合大小上，設計加工所有內容。
Canva automatically saves all your designs. This is beneficial because you can access your design or past designs anywhere you go. You do not have to save your design to a flash drive or be restricted to only using a certain computer. This also means you do not need to worry if your computer malfunctions or shuts down because your hard work will be safe.
然而我認為最方便的功能就是，Canva會自動保存所有設計，這真的很方便，因為可以隨時隨地編輯或加工過去的設計作品， 不必擔心作品會因為沒有儲存成功而必須重做，其保障所有對於設計上的細微改變，都會被完整地儲存。

Clip Studio Paint


Clip Studio Paint is a perfect place to start if you're taking your first steps into comic drawing and illustration. It's often optimized for use with mouse or digital pen, and if you're a professional comic artist, it's likely you'll be using these methods anyway.
如果您你想要開始製作漫畫和插圖，Clip Studio Paint是一個十分理想的起點，與滑鼠或者電子筆配合使用，可以讓功能效果更加豐富，如果你想成為專業的漫畫家，這軟體和配備時無可或缺的。
It has an excellent variety of paint tools and color options, access to a free library of over 10,000 assets, and it's easy to use - especially if you're familiar with the newer versions of PhotoShop. The power of this program, combined with the relatively great price, means it sits high on our list of the best comic creator software packages.
它具有多種繪畫工具和顏色選項，可以使用超過10,000多種素材的免費資料庫，並且如果你曾使用過繪圖軟體，Clip Studio Paint算是一個容易上手的，強大多元的功能以及相對較高的價格，使其在漫畫創作者的愛好軟體列表中位居前列。
