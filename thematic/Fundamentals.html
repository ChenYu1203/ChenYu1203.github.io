Graphic design is a craft where professionals create visual content to communicate messages.
平面設計是利用視覺內容來傳達訊息

By applying visual hierarchy and page layout techniques, designers use typography and pictures to meet users’ specific needs and focus on the logic of displaying elements in interactive designs, to optimize the user experience.
透過視覺層次以及頁面結構，利用圖片和排版美化後，滿足用戶的需求，此外，注重與用戶的互動性也是平面設計上非常重要的一環。

The fundamentals of graphic design are about being able to decide which material and layout-work are relevant, engaging and useful for visualizing a particular idea or solving a certain problem.
平面設計的基礎不外乎就是懂的將素材以及技術結合，創造出能表達想法或解決問題的作品。

When it comes to graphic design, we can definitely be creative, but we also have to be balanced. Balance and alignment is the basics of graphic design. Think of it like this: if you were decorating your living room, you wouldn’t try to squeeze the sofa, the recliner chair, the coffee table, and the end tables all into a tiny corner, right? No, you’d spread the pieces throughout the room to create balance and alignment. With graphic design, it’s exactly the same concept.
當我們講到視覺設計，創意力絕對是不可或缺的，但畫面的平衡更是關鍵。這跟空間設計的概念是一樣的，我們搬家時，不會把傢俱縮小或全部擺在角落，如何讓同一個空間看起來平衡且舒服，是身為設計師的基本任務。

On the other hand, color is also critical for graphic designs. Color drives engagement. But only if you know how to use it. The colors you choose for your graphic designs are crucial not only to your overall aesthetic, but to how well your designs connect with your audience. Different colors drive different results. When you’re choosing colors for your main color, you need to be sure the colors you choose are going to drive the effects you want.
另外，色彩更是決定視覺設計的關鍵要素，顏色可以讓觀眾更理解你的美學理念，因此與你的作品產生共鳴，不同的色彩搭配將會產生不同的效果，所以顏色的選擇將會取決於作品整體給人的印象。

Graphic designers must justify stylistic choices regarding image locations and font with a human-centered approach. That means you need to focus on and seek to empathize the most with your specific users while you create good-looking designs that maximize usability.
平面設計師必須用較主觀的角度，去選擇關於設計所有方面的細節，包含字體或者圖像位置。換句話說，針對不同用戶群，讓作品達到最大的效果以及影響力，是身為平面設計師一個關鍵的任務。
